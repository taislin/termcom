TERM=COM

A faithful demake of X-COM: UFO Defense (1994, MicroProse) rendered entirely in coloured ASCII on a terminal. Written in Go with tcell.

Overview

TERM=COM brings the classic alien invasion strategy experience to your terminal. It features a complete implementation of all gameplay loops: the Geoscape (global strategy), Base management, and the tactical Battlescape.

Key Features

  • Geoscape: Real-time global map with time compression and UFO tracking.
  • Battlescape: Turn-based tactical combat with Time Units, destructible terrain, and line-of-sight.
  • Base Management: Build facilities, hire soldiers, and equip your squad.
  • Deep Systems: Research alien technology, manage manufacturing, and track soldier progression.
  • Advanced AI & VFX: Intelligent alien behavior, volumetric smoke, and dynamic particle effects.
